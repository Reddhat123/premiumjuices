Our Story

Fresh Squeeze started with a simple mission: to provide the community with delicious, nutrient-rich juices that nourish the body and delight the taste buds.

Founded in 2015 by health enthusiasts Sarah and Mike, we've grown from a small farmer's market stand to a beloved local establishment, but our commitment to quality hasn't changed.

We source only the freshest organic fruits and vegetables from local farms, ensuring that every sip of our juice contains maximum nutrition and flavor.

Why Choose Fresh Squeeze?
